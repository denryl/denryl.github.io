PAVLOV CLASSICAL CONDITIONING

Pavlovian theory is a learning procedure that involves pairing a stimulus with a conditioned response (Stimulus "Timer") (Response "Answering"). When the user sees the timer they will know that it indicates that they have to answer it before the time runs out.
